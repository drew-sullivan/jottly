Frequently asked

How do I play?

Pick a secret five-letter word; your opponent does too. Take turns guessing. Each guess shows your jots - the number of your letters that appear in their secret word. Use those clues to deduce their word, and be the first to guess it exactly.

How do I play with a friend?

Tap New game, choose your secret word, then Invite a friend and share the link. Your friend opens it, picks their own secret word, and the duel begins - take your turns whenever you like.

No friend around, or want to practice?

Tap New game → Train with JotBot to play the computer any time, or take the daily challenge that everyone gets the same word for.

I'm not getting turn notifications.

Make sure notifications are enabled for Jottly in iOS Settings → Notifications → Jottly.

How do I restore a supporter purchase?

Open Settings in the app and tap Restore Purchases. Avatars are the only purchase - cosmetic only, and they never affect gameplay. Reactions are free for everyone.

How do I remove a game or my data?

Swipe a game on the Home screen to delete it. There's no account - uninstalling the app removes its local data. See our Privacy Policy for details.
