How do I play?

Pick a secret five-letter word; your opponent does too. Take turns guessing. Each guess shows your jots — the number of your letters that appear in their secret word. Use those clues to deduce their word, and be the first to guess it exactly.

How do I play with a friend?

Tap New game, choose your secret word, then Invite a friend and share the link. They open it, pick their own secret word, and the duel begins.

Can I practice solo?

Yes — tap New game → Train with JotBot any time, or take the daily challenge everyone shares.

I’m not getting turn notifications.

Enable notifications for Jottly in iOS Settings → Notifications → Jottly.

How do I restore a supporter purchase?

Open Settings in the app and tap Restore Purchases. Avatars are the only purchase — cosmetic only, and they never affect gameplay. Reactions are free for everyone.

How do I remove a game or my data?

Swipe a game on the Home screen to delete it. There’s no account — uninstalling the app removes its local data.

Privacy

Privacy Policy

Effective June 21, 2026

Jottly is made by Drew Sullivan. The short version: we built Jottly to be played and shared, not to harvest your data.

Our promise
  • No ads.
  • No tracking. We don’t track you across apps or websites, and use no advertising or analytics SDKs.
  • No account or sign-up. No email, password, or profile required to play.
  • No subscriptions, and nothing sold. We never sell or rent your information.

Information we don’t collect

We do not collect your name, email, contacts, location, advertising identifier, or usage analytics. Jottly has no login and integrates no third-party advertising, attribution, or analytics services.

Information used to play

To make a two-player word game work, some information is created on your device and, for games with a friend, synced through Apple’s iCloud / CloudKit so both players stay in sync:

  • Gameplay content — your secret word, guesses, reactions, streaks, and your display name — a random fun handle generated for you (there's no sign-in or account, and nothing to manage). For a game with a friend, this is shared with that opponent so the game can be played.
  • A push token — if you allow notifications, Apple provides a device token used only to tell you when it’s your turn. You can turn notifications off any time in iOS Settings.

This data lives on your device and in Apple’s iCloud infrastructure under Apple’s privacy policy. We don’t run our own servers and don’t use this content for advertising, profiling, or tracking.

Purchases

Jottly is free, and reactions are free for everyone. The only optional purchase is a JotBot avatar for your player tile — $0.99 each, or $4.99 to unlock all of them — handled entirely by Apple’s App Store. Apple processes the payment; we never receive your payment-card or billing details.

If you contact us

If you email us, we’ll receive your email address and message, and use them only to read and respond. We don’t add you to any list.

Children

Jottly suits a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. With no account and no data collection, no personal profile of any player is built.

Data retention and deletion

Game data stays until you delete a game in the app or remove the app. Deleting the app removes its local data; iCloud game data follows Apple’s iCloud handling for your account.

Changes

If this policy changes, we’ll update the date above and post the revised version here.
