Privacy Policy

Effective June 25, 2026

Hi! JotBot here. Here's the short version: we built Jottly to be played and shared, not to harvest your data.

Jottly is a word game made by Drew Sullivan at Iced Matcha Labs ("we," "us"). This policy explains what information the app does - and does not - involve.

Our promise
  • No ads.
  • No tracking. We don't track you across apps or websites, and we use no advertising or analytics SDKs.
  • No account or sign-up. No email, password, or profile is required to play.
  • Nothing sold. We never sell or rent your information.

Information we don't collect

We do not collect your name, email, contacts, location, advertising identifier, or usage analytics. Jottly has no login and integrates no third-party advertising, attribution, or analytics services.

Information used to play

To make a two-player word game work, some information is created on your device and, for games with a friend, synced through Apple's iCloud / CloudKit so both players stay in sync:

  • Gameplay content - your secret word, moves, streaks, and your display name - a playful handle by default, which you can change to your own name or keep anonymous (there's no sign-in or account, and nothing to manage). For a game with a friend, this is shared with that opponent so the game can be played.
  • A push token - if you allow notifications, Apple provides a device token used only to tell you when it's your turn. You can turn notifications off any time in iOS Settings.

This data lives on your device and in Apple's iCloud infrastructure under Apple's privacy policy. We do not run our own servers and do not use this content for advertising, profiling, or tracking.

Purchases

Jottly is free. The only optional purchase is a JotBot avatar for your player tile - $0.99 each, or $4.99 to unlock all of them - handled entirely by Apple's App Store. Buying one is just a friendly way to support the app. Apple processes the payment; we never receive your payment-card or billing details.

If you contact us

If you email us feedback, we'll receive your email address and whatever you write, and use it only to read and respond. We don't add you to any list.

Children

Jottly is suitable for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because there's no account and no data collection, no personal profile of any player is built.

Data retention and deletion

Game data stays until you delete a game in the app or remove the app. Deleting the app removes its local data; iCloud game data follows Apple's iCloud handling for your account.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update the date above and post the revised version at this URL.
